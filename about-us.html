Company Overview

Tama Group at a Glance

Founded in 1990, Tama Group has established itself as a leading provider of specialized healthcare solutions in Egypt and beyond. We focus on three core business areas: pharmaceutical excipients, active pharmaceutical ingredients (APIs), and sanitization solutions through our Mediclean line.

Our company combines global expertise with local market knowledge to deliver high-quality products that meet the evolving needs of healthcare providers and manufacturers. With a strong commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction, we have built enduring relationships with partners across the healthcare sector.
