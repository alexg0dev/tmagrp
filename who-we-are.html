Who We Are

Our Purpose

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, innovative healthcare solutions that improve the quality of life for patients and enable healthcare providers to deliver better care. We are committed to excellence, integrity, and continuous improvement in all that we do.

Our Vision

To be the leading healthcare solutions provider in the Middle East and North Africa, recognized for our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction. We envision a future where our products and services contribute significantly to advancing healthcare outcomes across the region.

Our Journey

Our History

Discover the key milestones that have shaped Tama Group's journey since our founding in 1990.

1990

Foundation

Tama Group was founded in Cairo, Egypt with a vision to provide high-quality healthcare solutions to the Egyptian market.

1995

First International Partnership

Established our first international partnership with a European pharmaceutical excipient manufacturer, expanding our product offerings.

2000

Market Expansion

Expanded our market reach to include several countries in North Africa and the Middle East, becoming a regional player in healthcare solutions.

2005

Quality Certification

Received ISO certification for our quality management systems, affirming our commitment to excellence and international standards.

2010

Product Line Expansion

Introduced our Mediclean line of products, diversifying our portfolio to include specialized sanitization solutions for healthcare facilities.

2015

Research & Innovation

Established our research and development department to focus on innovation and developing proprietary formulations.

2020

Pandemic Response

Rapidly expanded our Mediclean line and supply chain capabilities to support healthcare providers during the global pandemic.

Today

Looking Forward

Continuing to innovate and expand, with a focus on sustainable healthcare solutions and digital transformation to better serve our clients and partners.
